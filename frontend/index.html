Smart Money Method Australia — Free Financial Tools

A complete suite of financial tools designed for Australians. Model your income, plan your budget, simulate investment strategies, and trade with confidence — all in one free platform.

Australian Pay Calculator

Work out your exact take-home pay after Australian income tax, Medicare levy, and superannuation contributions. Supports weekly, fortnightly, and monthly pay cycles. Essential for anyone starting a new job, negotiating a salary, or planning a budget around their net pay.

Budget Planner

Plan your monthly budget by tracking income and expenses across multiple categories including housing, food, transport, entertainment, savings, and investments. Visualise where your money goes each month and identify opportunities to save more or redirect funds toward financial goals. The Smart Money Method budget planner is built for Australian households and living costs.

Money Compounding Calculator

See the power of compound interest over time. Enter a starting amount, regular contribution amount, interest rate, and investment horizon to project how your wealth grows. Whether you are investing in a high-interest savings account, ETFs, or ASX shares, compounding is the foundation of long-term wealth creation for Australians.

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) Calculator

Simulate a dollar cost averaging strategy for any ASX stock or ETF using real historical price data. Enter your regular contribution amount, start and end date, and the ASX ticker code to see how a consistent investment schedule would have grown over time. Dollar cost averaging reduces the impact of market volatility and is a proven strategy for long-term ASX investors.

Trading Profit / Loss Calculator

Calculate your exact profit or loss on any Australian share trade. Enter your entry price, exit price, number of shares or units, and brokerage fees to see your net return instantly. Supports both long and short trades and accounts for Australian brokerage commissions from platforms like CommSec, SelfWealth, Stake, and IG.

ASX Chart and Stock Screener

View live price charts for any ASX-listed share or ETF. The chart and screener tool integrates TradingView charts and allows you to search any ASX ticker code, overlay technical indicators, and analyse price action. Filter and screen ASX 300 stocks and Australian ETFs by price, market cap, sector, and performance.

Paper Trading Simulator — Trading Grounds

Practice share trading with no real money at risk. The Trading Grounds simulator gives you AUD $10,000 in virtual capital to trade over 6 rounds on a live candlestick chart. Go LONG (buy) or SHORT (sell) on each round and try to maximise your final portfolio value. Compete on the leaderboard against other traders. Add technical indicators including SMA 20, SMA 50, SMA 200, RSI, and MACD to inform your decisions.

ASX Backtesting Tool

Replay historical ASX price data and test your trading strategy with paper trades. Jump to any historical period, add indicators, execute virtual buy and sell orders at historical prices, and track your hypothetical results. Backtesting is used by professional traders to validate strategies before committing real capital.

Technical Indicators Guide

Learn how professional traders use technical indicators to identify entry and exit points on the ASX. Includes detailed explanations of Simple Moving Averages (SMA 20, SMA 50, SMA 200), Bollinger Bands, Relative Strength Index (RSI), Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP), Average True Range (ATR), Darvas Box Theory, and Fibonacci Retracement levels.
